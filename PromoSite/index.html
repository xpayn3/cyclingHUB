Open source & private

Your data stays yours.

CycleIQ runs entirely in your browser. No tracking, no analytics, no servers collecting your training data. Everything is stored locally in IndexedDB and localStorage.

Privacy First

Zero telemetry. No cookies. No fingerprinting. Your training data never leaves your device.

Fully Open Source

Every line of code is public. Audit it, fork it, contribute. No vendor lock-in ever.

Zero Dependencies

No React. No Vue. No npm install. Pure vanilla JS, HTML, and CSS — nothing to break.
