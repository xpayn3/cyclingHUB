Earn XP. Level Up.
Every ride earns XP from 8 scoring categories. Ride longer, climb harder, push your heart rate — every effort counts.
How XP Works
Each ride scores XP across 8 categories. Hit 4+ categories well and earn a combo multiplier (up to 1.35×). Streaks and consistency earn bonus XP on top.
Ride Scoring
Distance3–10 XP/km
Climbing1 XP per 4–15m
Duration0.5–1.5 XP/min
Training Load0.4–1.0 XP/TSS
Heart RateEffort × duration
Higher tiers unlock at longer distances, more climbing, and harder efforts. Each category has 4–5 progressive tiers.
Bonus XP
Speed Bonus+5 to +60 XP
Power Bonus+3 to +40 XP
Calorie Burn+5 to +30 XP
Ride Completed+10 XP always
Combo Multiplier
Well-rounded rides that score across multiple categories earn a multiplier bonus:
4 categories = 1.15× 5 categories = 1.25× 6 categories = 1.35×
Streaks & Consistency
Week Streak+5 XP/week + milestones
ConsistencyUp to +200 XP/year
VolumeUp to +500 XP/year
Streak milestones: 2 weeks (+15), 1 month (+60), 2 months (+150), 3 months (+300), 6 months (+800), 1 year (+2000). Ride at least once per week to keep your streak alive.
Example Rides
Easy 20 km flat~85 XP
60 km / 500 m / 2h~350 XP
100 km / 1500 m / 4h~750 XP
200 km / 3000 m / 8h~2,200 XP
XP values include heart rate and combo multiplier for typical efforts. Your actual XP may vary based on power, HR, and speed.
Leveling Curve
Level 141 XP (1 easy ride)
Level 10460 XP (~5 rides)
Level 251,375 XP (~15 rides)
Level 503,500 XP (~40 rides)
Level 10010,040 XP (~100 rides)
Level 20032,040 XP (~300 rides)
Level 500170,040 XP (max level)
Formula: 40n + 0.6n² XP per level. Early levels are fast, later ones require serious dedication. Max level is 500.
